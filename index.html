Bringing you the best car leasing deals of 2023

More and more drivers are choosing to lease a car, as a savvy way to drive a brand new car for a fraction of the price. Here at Hippo Leasing, we have 1000’s of car leasing deals to choose from.

Whether you’re looking for a business lease deal or a car for personal use, we’re able to offer you the best deals on a wide range of vehicles. We stock cars and vans from Ford, BMW, Audi and more – and if we don’t have the car you’re looking for, we’ll source it for you.

We’re also specialist credit brokers and we work with a diverse panel of lenders, so you can still lease a car with bad credit.

So why choose Hippo Leasing?

  • We have 1000s of new and used vehicles to choose from
  • If we don’t have the car you’re looking for, we’ll source it for you
  • We have options for customers with bad credit
  • There’s a no deposit option, so you pay nothing for 30 days
  • All our used cars go through a thorough 130-point check before being leased
  • We offer nationwide delivery straight to your door
